# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Primary:** Agro sector professionals and brands — companies, cooperatives, event organizers, and marketing teams evaluating Recanto Gobbo as a partner for speaking engagements, branded activations, ambassador programs, or event proposals. They arrive with a business intent and assess credibility first, warmth second.

**Secondary:** Consumers who identify with rural and campo culture — people who follow the brand's creator presence on social media and want to deepen the relationship through products, events, or direct connection.

**Context:** Both groups arrive from social media. The site is often the first structured, non-feed experience they have with the brand. First impressions carry full weight.

## Product Purpose

Recanto Gobbo is a creator-led rural lifestyle brand built on a genuine identity: a person or family whose life on the land became a public presence, then a business. The brand offers products (artisanal caps), services (lectures, digital ambassador programs, brand activations, event proposals), and an invitation to a worldview rooted in the campo.

The site's job is to translate that lived identity into a professional surface without sanitizing it. A visiting brand manager must leave confident enough to send a proposal. A fan must leave feeling the connection is real.

Success looks like: partnerships signed, engagements booked, and a site that would embarrass neither a rural family proud of their heritage nor a marketing director preparing to present it to a client.

## Brand Personality

**Four words:** Autêntico, Acolhedor, Orgulhoso, Profissional.

Autêntico and Orgulhoso set the identity: genuinely rooted in rural life, proud of the campo and the work without apology or affectation. Acolhedor sets the register: warm enough to welcome anyone curious, not just insiders. Profissional sets the floor: credible and reliable enough to be trusted with a brand's name and an audience's attention.

The voice is direct and warm. It does not perform humility about rural life, nor does it over-explain it to urban audiences. It assumes the campo is worth knowing and invites the visitor in.

## Anti-references

**Influencer genérico:** Beige aesthetic, curated flat-lays, 'slow living' minimalism, soft sans-serif everywhere. This is a lifestyle pose, not an identity. It looks like ten thousand other accounts. Recanto Gobbo has a specific place, specific people, and a specific life; the design must carry that specificity.

**Agronegócio corporativo:** Slick, impersonal agribusiness aesthetics. Machinery brochures, investor decks dressed as websites, navy-and-gold palettes signaling commodities. This brand is human, not industrial. No suits-and-charts energy.

## Design Principles

1. **Identidade antes de estética.** Design choices serve a real, specific identity. Anything that looks "designed" but feels disconnected from the land, the people, or the work is wrong by definition. Specificity over elegance.

2. **Confiança profissional com calor genuíno.** The primary visitor is evaluating a business partner. The brand must be credible and serious, but never cold or corporate. Professionalism here means earned trust, not formal distance.

3. **O campo como identidade, não como cenário.** Rural references must feel lived-in, not decorative. The campo is the source of the brand's identity — not a backdrop, not a prop, not an aesthetic borrowed for warmth.

4. **O ritmo da terra, não da loja.** Generous spacing, unhurried pacing, and calm surfaces signal a brand of substance. The design does not rush the visitor. Nothing about this site should feel like a conversion funnel.

5. **Escassez como eloquência.** Accent colors, emphasis, and visual intensity appear rarely and earn their place by contrast with the calm around them. The quieter the surface, the louder the moments that break it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

**Target:** WCAG 2.1 AA.

- Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text, 3:1 for large text and UI components.
- All interactive elements keyboard-navigable with visible focus indicators.
- Meaningful `alt` text on images, especially hero photography (alt text carries the same voice as the brand copy).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for any transitions and animations.
- Touch targets minimum 44×44px on mobile surfaces.
